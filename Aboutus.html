About Us

Founded in 2010 by a single entrepreneur, GROCO has over the past 10 years grown exponentially to capture a wide area in srilanka. The company has its main branch located in Colombo and others are in Kandy,Matara & Nuwara Eliya.This provides our customers with a world class retail experience.

If you are looking for farm fresh fruits and vegetables, world-class breads, top-quality pulses and food grains, dairy products, then, GROCO has got you covered. No more getting stuck in traffic jams, paying for parking, standing in long queues and carrying heavy bags- get everything you need, when you need, right at your doorstep. Right from fresh fruits and vegetables, rice and dals, spices and seasonings to packaged products-we have it all. Choose from a wide range of options in every category, exclusively handpicked to help you find the best quality available at lowest prices.
